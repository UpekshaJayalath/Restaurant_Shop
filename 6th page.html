Hamburger

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a dish made up of a patty of ground meat—typically beef—that is sandwiched between two slices of bread. Hamburgers are commonly placed on sesame seed buns and frequently come with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, or chilis. They may also come with ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, relish, or a "special sauce," which is frequently a variant of Thousand Island dressing..
